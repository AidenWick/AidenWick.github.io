Welcome

Hi, welcome to my website! Feel free to take a look around and explore!

In this class, Universal Design, I learned about way more effective ways to build a website. You can build a website in an easier way and have it be more accessible by everyone, no matter their ability or skills. This also has a huge impact on search engine optimization, so you are more likely to get more people and attention on your website. Overall universal design is just about one of the smartest things to do for your website.
